Gaussian blur

Blurring an image by a Gaussian function. It is used to reduce image noise and reduce detail.

Corner detection

A corner can be defined as the intersection of two edges.

Optical character recognition

Optical character recognition is the mechanical or electronic conversion of images of typed, handwritten or printed text into machine-encoded text.

Image segmentation

In computer vision, image segmentation is the process of partitioning a digital image into multiple segments.
